HISTORY

Farbucks was founded in December 2021, founded by two people who wanted to create new innovations in serving coffee. They also want to present a cafe place with a concept that looks peaceful with pastel green shades. The difference between Farbucks and other cafes is that they provide a special room for students who want to do their schoolwork.

VISI & MISI

VISI

Making Farbucks a place for teenagers or coffee lovers to learn to serve coffee with new innovations all the time. Not only that, I would like to introduce that local coffee brands can also co-exist with well-known coffee brands.

MISI

1. Always put the customer first.
2. Prioritizing customer convenience and coffee quality.
3. Provide a safe place for everyone.
4. Motivate employees to remain committed to carrying out their work.
